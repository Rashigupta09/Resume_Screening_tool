Senior Backend Engineer

About the role
We are hiring a Senior Backend Engineer to own critical server-side systems that power
our payments platform. This is a hands-on, senior role with significant ownership.

Required (must-have)
- 5+ years building and operating production backend services at scale.
- Strong experience designing and running RESTful APIs and event-driven services.
- Deep working knowledge of relational databases (PostgreSQL) including query optimization.
- Experience owning services in production: on-call, incident response, observability.
- Proficiency in Python (or a comparable backend language) and solid testing discipline.

Strongly preferred
- Experience with cloud infrastructure (AWS) and infrastructure-as-code.
- Exposure to payments, billing, or other high-reliability financial systems.

Nice to have
- Familiarity with message queues (Kafka, SQS) and caching layers (Redis).
- Mentoring or technical-leadership experience.

What success looks like
You will be the primary owner of two production services, drive their reliability and
performance, and raise the engineering bar through code review and mentorship.
